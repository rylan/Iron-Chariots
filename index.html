Welcome.

The Iron Chariots Wiki provides information on apologetics and counter-apologetics by collecting common arguments and providing responses. A goal of the site is to help counter misinformation and poor arguments which masquerade as evidence for religious claims.

Supported Systems.

This application is currently designed for mobile and tablet specific experience for accessing the Iron Chariots Wiki found at http://wiki.ironchariots.org. Currently supported systems are webOS, Android (ICS tablet only), Windows Phone 7 and BlackBerry Playbook.
